Hi 👋 My name is João Brandão
=============================

Software Engineer
-----------------

Having over four years of experience in software development, I have built a solid and diverse career that spans from Python programming for web development and RPA to specializing in JavaScript, a language in which I have been working for three years. Currently, my focus is on backend development with Node.js and software engineering, where I dedicate myself to creating dynamic and efficient solutions. Despite my focus on the backend, I also frequently use Angular and React in my projects, demonstrating my flexibility and skill in both development fronts. I am passionate about solving complex problems using technology as a foundation, a passion that began even before starting college in Computer Science. As a JavaScript Developer, I pride myself on my ability to optimize and map custom systems, helping companies to improve their processes and efficiency. I provide ongoing support to enterprise systems and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow professionally. I firmly believe that programming is more than just learning a language; it's about learning to solve problems efficiently and innovatively, within the client's stack and regardless of the technology used.
